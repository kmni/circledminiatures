About Us

Welcome to Circle D Miniatures

We are located in the heart of Northwest Ohio...

Being fairly new to the miniature horse industry, we are proud to claim some of the highest quality and most successful horses in the Midwest. Owned by Landin and Derick Doner, Darin Doner, who formerly raised draft horses and mules, and Chris Doner, who formerly raised and trained champion Arabian horses and three day event Thoroughbreds, Circle D Miniatures was established with a unique vision deriving from two diverse backgrounds. All of the horses on our farm have been chosen for their superb conformation and champion-rich pedigrees to produce present and future champions.

Established in 2010, we began with one very special mare, Little Kings BT Destiny Daisy. "Daisy" has been the horse of a life time with multiple supreme titles, two consecutive world titles, and earning the title of 2011 World Reserve Grand Champion Amateur Senior Mare. With such a successful show record, Daisy has set the standards high for the future horses at Circle D Miniatures.

Our horses consist of the bloodlines of the following world renowned stallions from across the world: NFC's Superman, McCarthy's Aloha Silent Partner, Sids Rebel, Little Kings Black Velvet, Boones Little Buckeroo, Alvadars Double Destiny, Ima Boones Little Buckeroo Too, Reeces Thunder Hawk, Lucky Four Galahads Impressive, Lucky Four Rebels Chasin Rainbows, First Knights Bit O Hot Shot, and Kewpie's Klassic Kreation of Arenosa to highlight a few.

We are a boutique breeding program raising approximately 3-4 foals each year. A strict policy of quality not quantity is followed to insure each horse receives individualized attention each day. With this theory in mind, our goal is to raise a foal that will compete at the highest level in halter and performance. Our horses compete annually at local, regional, national, and world level shows across the country with us and our friends at Aloha Acres Training Center.

We invite you to browse our website and view our collection of world class miniature horses!
