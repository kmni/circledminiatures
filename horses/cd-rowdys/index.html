CD Rowdy's She Be Jammin

Lucky Four Rebelchase Real Rowdy x Lucky Four Black Velvet Honeybuck

Details

Foaled: 4/28/201333.50"AMHA/AMHR Registered
AMHA Futurity Nominated
Parentage Qualified

Firecracker! Hot! This remarkable filly, who is a ? sister to our multiple supreme champion CD Rebelchase Once Upon A Showtime, has an attitude like no other. "Jamie" has extreme presence and beautiful motion - she floats around wherever she goes. Besides her demanding presence, this filly exhibits a solid body structure, great tail carriage, an arched and hooky neck, and a breathtaking face which is short from eye to muzzle. Her pedigree combines the "best of the best" from Lucky Four Farms. With the combination of her sire and dam, "Jamie" has three crosses back to Sids Rebel (not to mention Little Kings Black Velvet)!
