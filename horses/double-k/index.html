Our Mares

Double K's She's A Sassy Kid

Willowlawn's Foxxy Kid (3X HOF) x Double K's Shiloh of Establo

Details

Foaled: 5/13/12Under 30"AMHR and ASPC Registered

Show Highlights

  • 2013 6X Supreme Champion at the ECMHC Spring Fling
  • 2012 National Champion Futurity Mare Foal, 30" & Under (Out of 50++ entries)

*Co-owned with Aloha Acres Training Center

 

A wonderful contribution to our program, "Loretta" was our very first AMHR/ASPC show filly. She is sired by the legendary National GRAND Champion Halter and Performance Stallion Willowlawn's Foxxy Kid. Her dam, Double K's Shiloh of Establo, is a National Champion Halter Mare herself. "Loretta's" extended pedigree blends the best of both the Kid and the Arenosa bloodlines. With a pedigree that speaks for itself, "Loretta" followed in her sire and dam's footsteps and was crowned the 2012 National Champion Futurity Mare Foal, 30" & Under (Out of 50++ entries) with Lee Crutchfield on the lead! Along with many supreme wins and National Top Tens in 2013, she is now retired from the show arena and preparing for motherhood.
