A very flashy pinto filly, Humhills Miss B Haven is the result of a strategic cross between a Reeces Thunder Hawk son and a Sids Rebel daughter. She exemplifies a beautiful Arabian style head, femininity, a correct body, and a neck/shoulder with superior length and shape. Now that she is retired from an extended show career, we are anxiously waiting to breed her to our state-of-the-art stallion, McCarthy's Kryptonite, for a 2015 show stopper!
