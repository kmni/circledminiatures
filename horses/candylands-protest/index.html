The Geldings

Candylands Protest This Investment

Westwind Farms Spunky Attitude X Candylands Justa Dreaming

Details

Foaled: 4/18/2009 32.50"AMHA/AMHR Registered

Presented in 2014 by Circle D Miniatures
Senior Geldings, Over 32"-34"
Amateur w/ Darin Doner
Youth w/ Landin Doner

 

"Protest" is no stranger to the show arena! This impressive and spunky gelding was the 2010 World GRAND Champion Junior Gelding with Lee Crutchfield on lead. As a senior gelding, he has gone on to be a 2X National Reserve GRAND Champion Senior Gelding, Under, a multiple youth National/World Champion, a multiple Supreme Champion, and a multiple Regional Grand Champion Senior Gelding. He is showing great longevity in the show arena and will soon begin his performance training.
