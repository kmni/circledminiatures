The heart and soul of our farm and our very first miniature show horse, Little Kings BT Destiny Daisy set the standards high when we established a program. Universally admired by the most respected breeders and judges around the world, "Daisy" has surpassed all of our expectations by receiving some of the highest honors in the show arena available. We believe that she was "destined" for greatness! "Daisy" is the epitome of a classic miniature horse with a show attitude that won't quit, a sleek topline, naturally fit body, pencil legs, a long extended neck, and a refined face with tippy ears.

 

Her sire is the acclaimed World Grand Champion Senior Stallion Alvadars Double Destiny who is known around the world for his beauty and type. In 2011, "Daisy" had the honor to represent her sire by being a World Champion Get of Sire Participant! Her dam, Boones Little BTU Zephyroo, is a daughter of the prolific World Grand Champion Senior Stallion IMA Boones Little Buckeroo Too. "Daisy" is now retired from the show arena and is preparing for the next chapter in her life ? motherhood!
