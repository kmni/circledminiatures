FHF She's Bucking Believe It

Olive Branchs Buck Believe It x Freedom Hill Farms Echoing Illusion

Details

Foaled: 3/22/201033"AMHA/AMHR Registered
Parentage Qualified

**Currently in foal to McCarthy's Kryptonite for a 2014 show stopper**

 

A very animated mare, FHF She's Bucking Believe It loves nothing more than to be a show off! "Believe" flags her tail every time she goes into her naturally flowing and graceful trot. Her sire is a son of Alvadars Double Destiny - 2011 AMHA World Champion Get-Of-Sire, and producer of too many World Champions to list here. This mare's dam is a daughter of the prolific Little Kings Buck Echo as well as a granddaughter to the immortal Boones Little Buckeroo (that makes 3 World GRAND Champions in her tight pedigree). "Believe" is a balanced, correct mare with straight legs, good bite, excellent length of neck, naturally fit body, and a feminine head. She has a willing disposition and is easy to work with yet bright and inquisitive.
