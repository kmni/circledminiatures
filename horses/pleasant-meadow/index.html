Pleasant Meadows Buckalucy

Little Kings Buck King x PM Dancin in the Daisies

Details

Foaled: 4/19/199929.75"AMHA/AMHR Registered
Parentage Qualified

For her small stature, Pleasant Meadows Buckalucy is a prime of example of an "Arabian in miniature." Other than being a Reserve World Champion mare, "Lucy" has excellent proportionality with a beautifully dished face, a high neck set, and a solid body structure. Furthermore, her pedigree includes the great World Grand Champion stallions Boones Little Buckeroo and Skip A Star!
