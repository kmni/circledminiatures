Retained for our breeding program, this filly is sired by our herd sire McCarthy's Kryptonite!

"Kimmy" has a beautiful refined body, good length of neck, a small and refined muzzle, her sires perfect ears, and excellent motion. Her dam, RFM Rebels Firedance, is a Little Kings Black Velvet granddaughter along with two crosses back to Sids Rebel. This makes "Kimmy" a desirable Shetland/Miniature cross.
