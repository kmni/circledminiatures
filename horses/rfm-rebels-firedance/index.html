RFM Rebels Firedance

ML Bold Image x Lucky Four Rebels Red N Rosey

Details

Foaled: 5/21/2010 33"AMHA/AMHR Registered
Parentage Qualified

Progeny

  • CD Kryptonite's Kimdurah

**Currently in foal to McCarthy's Kryptonite for a 2014 show stopper**

 

Originally purchased to cross with our stallion, McCarthy's Kryptonite, RFM Rebels Firedance delivered a beautiful and delicate grey filly by him in 2013. "Firedance" is a promising young mare who displays the brilliance of a champion with an alert attitude, refinement, dished and refined head, high neck set, and overall correctness.

 

Her sire, ML Bold Image, is a son of Little Kings Black Velvet out of Lucky Four Rebels Rebelicious (a Sids Rebel daughter). Her dam, Lucky Four Rebels Red N Rosy (who now resides in Europe), is a world champion producing daughter of Sids Rebel. As a result, "Firedance" is a maternal sister to World Champion Ravenwood Medallion No Laffin Matter.
