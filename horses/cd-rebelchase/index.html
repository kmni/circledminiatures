CD Rebelchase Once Upon A Showtime is the very first miniature horse that was raised here at our farm, and yet again, this set the standards extremely high for the future horses of Circle D Miniatures. Long super model legs, an extremely high neck set complimented by a long arched neck, a naturally fit and rock solid body, a beautiful face with a sweet eye, elegant femininity, and the brilliant attitude that is a priority in the show arena today are just a few of the characteristics that make this mare a rare jewel. "Showtime" of course was no accident. Her sire, Lucky Four Rebels Chasin Rainbows (who now resides in Australia), is a World Champion Futurity Stallion. The combination of both her sire and dam make "Showtime" a Little Kings Black Velvet and Sids Rebel granddaughter as well as a Sids Rebel great-granddaughter!
