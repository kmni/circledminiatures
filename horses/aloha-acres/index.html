Aloha Acres A Silent Promise

McCarthy's Aloha Silent Partner x Flamingos Windy Cherokee Chief

Details

Aloha Acres A Silent Promise is a golden palomino daughter of the one and only, McCarthy's Aloha Silent Partner. She portrays extreme Arabian type with her chiseled face (which is complimented by tippy ears and big black eyes), a flat topline, and a neck with just the right amount of hook. We are eagerly waiting to see what she produces when crossed with our supreme herd sire McCarthy's Kryptonite!
