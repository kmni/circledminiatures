We would like to introduce our exquisite senior stallion, McCarthy's Kryptonite! A huge and appreciative "thank you" goes out to the McCarthy's of Lakeside Stables for giving us the opportunity to purchase this outstanding stallion. We are confident that "Kryptonite" will cross well with our mares to produce future champions that will continue to put our farm at the top of the miniature horse world. This stallion has already produced a fine line of champions in a very small number of foals. His most recent champion filly, Ravenwood Ever After, was named 2012 World Champion Weanling Mare, Over 26"-30" and 2013 World Champion Yearling Mare, Over 30"-32!"

 

A half-brother to McCarthy's Aloha Silent Partner of Aloha Acres, "Kryptonite" boasts some of the best bloodlines in miniature horses today. His sire, NFC's Superman, is a National GRAND Champion in both Halter and Roadster. His dam, Michigan's Pretty Penny, was a Reserve National GRAND Champion Halter Mare, Under. Along with this combination of champions producing champions, "Kryptonite" is a 50% Shetland bred stallion giving him that desirable "blend" in the miniature horses today.

 

We invite all inquiries for those interested in our phenomenal stallion! He will be standing at stud to a limited amount of approved mares in 2014. Please take the time to view his pictures and observe the quality that this stallion represents.
