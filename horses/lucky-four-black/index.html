Lucky Four Black Velvet Honeybuck

Little Kings Black Velvet x Lucky Four Rebels Honey Gold

Parentage Qualified

A great contribution to our breeding program, Lucky Four Black Velvet Honeybuck is a royally bred mare. Her sire, the legendary and iconic Little Kings Black Velvet, is one of the most well-known miniature horses in the industry and a multiple World Grand Champion sire who now resides in Spain. Her dam, Lucky Four Rebels Honey Gold, is a daughter of the legendary Sids Rebel, and she is a World Champion and World Champion producer. "Honey" has produced two outstanding fillies for us so far that mimic her type, quality, and presence. She is simply a classic
