Home to World Champion
Miniature Horses

Located in Northwestern Ohio, Circle D Miniatures all began with the dreams and inspirations of our son, Landin Doner.

Raised to ride trail horses and show Shetlands and mules, Landin went another direction upon seeing the miniatures at the county fair. The purchase of Jo Co Miniatures Pepper Ann started it all, for there was no turning back now! Our son has had a dream, and his trainer, friends, and family are making it all come true. Welcome to our new farm, Circle D Miniatures, established 2010. Quality horses, not quantity, is our present and future goal.

Circle D Miniatures is owned by Darin and Chris Doner. Darin began his equine career by showing national champion mules, and Chris grew up showing national champion Arabians and three day event Thoroughbreds. We are now a small family operation just beginning with our first show string and broodmares. Our bloodlines focus on: McCarthys Aloha Silent Partner, L&D Scout, Boones Little Buckeroo, Alvadars Double Destiny, Sids Rebel, Reeces Thunder Hawk, Rowdy, and Hemlock Brooks Egyptian King, and the Arenosa bloodlines to highlight a few. We are very excited to have our very first foals out in the show ring, and it has been made possible by some very special people. A very appreciative "thank you" goes out to Brian and Danielle Hill, Wade Burns, Jon Woodring, Lee Crutchfield, and Kirby Jones. Without them, we would not have these wonderful and beautiful horses. In addition, our family looks forward to showing with the friendly people we met during our first show season.
